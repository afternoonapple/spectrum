He finishes off his enigmatic remark by throwing his hands upwards. The wall folds inwards, revealing a large, circular machine with pods lining the outside and countless cables on the inside.

ZENITH: Well.

KRANYON: Okay.

DR. BROWN: Once I learned of the existence of the 16 of you a few years ago, I started building this machine as a tribute to science.

DR. BROWN: To push the limits of biological development into unknown realms.

DR. BROWN: And a week and a half ago, I finally finished it.

CANDELL: Yeah that’s cool and all, but what does it do?

DR. BROWN: You know how your power increases with the onset of puberty?

DR. BROWN: The power of the sun allows this machine to boost this potential to unprecedented levels.

DR. BROWN: In fact if all goes well it should double the gains from the different tiers.

MORAN: tiers?

CASSIOPEIA: oh, sorry doctor, we didn’t have enough time to go through the lesson on tiers just now!

DR. BROWN: Oh, okay.

DR. BROWN: Did you at least cover the aspects?

CASSIOPEIA: actually, yeah we did.

DR. BROWN: Well, that’s great.

DR. BROWN: You should be familiar with the fact that everyone usually has a distinct major and minor aspect, with some having such a weak minor aspect that they only ever use their major aspect their entire lives.

DR. BROWN: I, for one, am a light-matter bearer (as you know, the common naming code for bearers is major-minor).

DR. BROWN: But with you 16 kids, the difference in power is so much less. It’s like some of you don’t even have a clear major aspect!

DR. BROWN: That’s why I chose you.

GALINDA: Wow, okay

NEXUS: that’s quite creepy but also cool

DR. BROWN: That’s a pretty good question.

DR. BROWN: I have magic assessment records from elementary and middle schools across the country.

DR. BROWN: Through the years I tracked down 16 suitable candidates for this groundbreaking program.

ZENITH: Wait, so this has been in the works for years??

DR. BROWN: Well, yeah.

ZENITH: But for what?

DR. BROWN: We’ll get to that later.

ONNYX: What about the tiers?

ONNYX: What are those
