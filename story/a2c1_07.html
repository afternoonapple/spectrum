MORAN: uh.

MORAN: anyway, he went off to check the facilities in the lab about like half an hour ago

MORAN: what’s the status arwen?

ARWEN: All out of electricity.

ARWEN: The collapse of the building probably cut the lab’s access to the power grid.

ARWEN: The circuits in the equipment dealing with magic were completely fried.

ARWEN: The machine likely released a massive wave of magic energy into the building and who knows for how much further after that.

ATOM: So then we can’t get back to school by the teleporter.

ARWEN: Yeah, we’re gonna be stuck here for days until we can re-establish power.

ARWEN: Actually, probably at least a week.

FENFERRA: Oh no, that’s not good.

ARWEN: It could be, actually.

ARWEN: I found a few empty spare rooms in the lab, a kitchen area and a bulk pack of 20 sleeping bags.

ARWEN: So there’s plenty of space for everyone.

ARWEN: They also have those cool garden growth things where you can get pretty much any crop you want in a couple of hours.

FENFERRA: Ooh, so we get to cook whatever we want to eat?

ARWEN: I guess.

MORAN: wait, you’re saying we live here?

ARWEN: Well, uh…

ARWEN: Actually it’s entirely possible.

ATOM: I really don’t mind.

ATOM: Although, don’t you think we should wait for the others to wake up first, then make a decision?

MORAN: yeah we probably should

FENFERRA: Then what should we do now?

FENFERRA: The best course of action is to just wait, then.

ARWEN: Maybe that’s what we should do.

ARWEN: Oh yeah by the way, anyone saw Aurora?

ARWEN: The blue girl.

ARWEN: Her pod door was left slightly ajar, and when I peered closely through the one way glass there was no one.

MORAN: you mean she’s just gone??

MORAN: we should definitely look for her once we have enough people.

ATOM: In the meantime…

ATOM: Guess we’ll just wait.


And waiting was exactly what they did.
