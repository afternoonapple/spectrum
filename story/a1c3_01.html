Chapter Summary: The kids go on a harmless adventure.

At the school foyer, the kids are directed onto a large teleporter pad. They are taken to a remote laboratory in the countryside.


AURORA: Huh?

FENFERRA: I think we just got teleported here.

ADELPHI: Woah.

ATOM: Where even are we?

???????: I’m glad you asked.


Practically all the kids turn around to see a short, old man in a grey lab coat.


DR. BROWN: My full name is Ophiuchus Brown, but you kids can call me Doctor Brown.

DR. BROWN: Nice to meet you!

SAMSARA: woah, you’re like… an actual person?

NEFFERIN: we dont really see a lot of real adults around

DR. BROWN: But your past is not what I brought you here for.

DR. BROWN: I brought you here for your future.


He finishes off his enigmatic remark by throwing his hands upwards. The wall folds inwards, revealing a large, circular machine with pods lining the outside and countless cables on the inside.


ZENITH: Well.

DR. BROWN: To push the limits of biological development into realms unknown.

MORAN: tiers?

CASSIOPEIA: nope.

DR. BROWN: Well, I’ll just have to give you kids a crash course!
